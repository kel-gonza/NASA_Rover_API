Raw Images

What to Expect?

Welcome to the page for Spirit's Raw Images! Here, you can view raw photos that Spirit has taken on Mars on a specific date. Simply enter the date you're interested in, and you'll be able to see how many photos the rover took on that day. Additionally, you can choose to filter the photos by camera or see them all at once.

What is a Sol?

A sol is the Martian equivalent of a day. When you select a particular day on the rover's webpage, you'll also see the corresponding sol number for that day. The sol count begins on the day the rover landed on Mars. For instance, if a photo was taken on Spirit's 1000th Martian sol exploring Mars, its sol attribute would be 1000.

Camera Abbreviations

When you click on the filters, you'll see some unfamiliar words. These are abbreviations for the types of cameras that Spirit has. It's important to note that while Spirit and Opportunity have the same cameras, Curiosity has a different ones. So, don't forget to check the Curiosity rover's page to see what kind of camera it has.
